The game was developed with Unity game engine, which guarantees you realistic physics and as a result stunning impression.

In Flappy48 you have to find the way through endless gates with fluttering cube, having number inside, increases its meaning each time you collect the same number. If you collect divergent number, you get another cube following you as a tale, waiting for its pair to be collected. The aim is clear and simple enough - just get the best score!
