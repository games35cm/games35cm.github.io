Printer Pro is another app from Jagwinder Sighn, developers team, famous for its document adaptation applications. This app is dedicated to solve one of the most annoying problems which is faced by many people doing their daily activities namely - printing. Printer Pro does not refill ink nor add paper, but what it does, it makes sure that your printer prints what you want and how you want it to be done.


Tap anywhere to preview live screenshots fullscreen.


In addition this app can convert text files in PDF and supports such useful features as Dropbox, ITunes filesharing and Air printing. As a conclusion we can say that Printer Pro is a utility which you should have on your device if you are not searching for a hard way advisedly.
